MAPxyz - map files
MONxyz - monster files
TLKxyz - talk files

Values for x:
 X - Earth maps
 G - Galactic maps

Values for y on Earth maps:
 0 - Legends
 1 - Pangea
 2 - BC
 3 - AD
 4 - Aftermath

Values for y on Galactic maps:
 1 - Mercury
 2 - Venus
 3 - Mars
 4 - Jupiter
 5 - Saturn
 6 - Uranus
 7 - Neptune
 8 - Pluto
 9 - X

Values for z:
 0 - overworld
 1 - village
 2 - town
 3 - castle
 4 - tower
 5 - dungeon

There are no TLK files for towers and dungeons.
